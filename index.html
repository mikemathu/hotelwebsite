About Us

Watergate Hotel is a landmark and one of the most desirable holiday destinations Kilifi has to offer located at the center of Kilifi town next to the Kilifi post office, a two minutes walk to the district headquarters. A walking distance to the most famous Kilifi Bridge and the sandy beaches. All the 30 rooms are en-suite wall to wall tiles, wonderful balconies overlooking the town, with fans, T.V with DSTV channels and WiFi (wireless internet) connectivity at all points of the hotel .A conference Hall for 40people, two restaurants on the ground floor and another on the roof top together with a lobby .
